# Product

## Register

product

## Users

July Player is used by learners who watch downloaded course videos on a desktop app. They need quick playback controls, reliable subtitles, AI translation, notes, and pet companion features without visual clutter interrupting study.

## Product Purpose

The app helps users import local course folders, watch lessons, track progress, manage subtitles, translate content with AI, write notes, and keep a persistent pet companion. Success means the player feels stable, fast, readable, and easy to operate during long study sessions.

## Brand Personality

Calm, friendly, focused. The interface can feel soft and playful in pet moments, but core playback and settings surfaces should stay practical and predictable.

## Anti-references

Avoid crowded dashboards, oversized decorative panels, hard black overlays, cramped dropdowns, inconsistent form controls, and playful effects that make study workflows slower.

## Design Principles

- Keep learning controls scannable and stable.
- Use one consistent component language across settings, AI, playback, and pet pages.
- Make every action show clear feedback.
- Let playful pet features enhance the app without blocking core video tasks.
- Prefer restrained motion that communicates state changes.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Aim for readable contrast, keyboard-accessible controls, visible focus states, reduced-motion fallbacks, and layouts that do not overflow or overlap on common desktop and tablet widths.
